[IDH4035] Honors Seminar: Comics, Fandom, Cosplay

The Liminal Experience of Pensacon

This is a paper that attempts to contextualize the feeling of "unreality" I felt watching crowds at Pensacon 2024 within broader cultural contexts. It was written under the guidance of Dr. Kevin Scott, who helped me go from a loose network of connections I made during ideation to a linear, honed-in final draft.

[IDH4037] Honors Seminar: Non-Place

For the final project of this class, we had to pick a topic to discuss non-place in relation to. To do this, I made a video essay showcasing some ways in which different pieces of media themed around alienation relate to non-place. I consider this a decent practice run for a format I want to do more of in the future.
